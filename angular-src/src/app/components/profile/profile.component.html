For the Monthly Evaluative Essay Report, each of the following categories will be graded on a 1 to 6 scale:

Writing Conventions - Writer uses formal standard English and avoids banned words and overly informal language

Paragraph Coherence/Unity - All sentences are arranged in a logical order and belong in the paragraph

Sentence Variety - Sentence openers are not repetitive within a paragraph

Ideas/Depth/Length - Writer includes original ideas and does not settle for a superficial discussion of the topic; each paragraph is at least 8 sentences long


Note:

For Vocabulary, each student works at his/her own pace, so parents will need to check with the teacher if there are missing vocabulary assignments.

Essays are often broken up into parts, and only one part is due each week.

When fractions are used, the denominator represents the total number of assignments given, and the numerator represents the number of assignments completed and turned in.
